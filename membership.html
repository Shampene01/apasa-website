Frequently Asked Questions

Who can become a member?

Any registered accommodation provider in South Africa is eligible for membership.

What are the membership fees?

We offer different tiers of membership with annual fees ranging from R2,500 to R5,000, with custom pricing for corporate members.

How long does membership last?

Membership is valid for one year from the date of approval and can be renewed annually.
