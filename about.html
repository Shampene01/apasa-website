About APASA

Leading the way in South African accommodation provision

Our Story

APASA was established to represent and advance the interests of accommodation providers across South Africa. We serve as a unified voice for the industry, working closely with government bodies, business stakeholders, and tenant representatives to create a sustainable and thriving accommodation sector.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To advance the interests of Accommodation Providers in South Africa through:

  • Effective stakeholder engagement
  • Industry leadership and advocacy
  • Professional development support
  • Knowledge sharing and best practices

Our Vision

To create a thriving and sustainable accommodation sector that:

  • Promotes excellence in service delivery
  • Ensures fair and ethical business practices
  • Contributes to economic growth
  • Supports community development

Our Key Stakeholders

Government

Working with local and national government bodies to shape policies and regulations that support the accommodation sector.

Tenants

Promoting fair and transparent relationships between providers and tenants while ensuring quality accommodation standards.

Business Partners

Collaborating with industry partners to create opportunities and drive innovation in the accommodation sector.

Leadership Team

Executive Committee

Our executive committee comprises industry leaders with extensive experience in property management and accommodation services.

Advisory Board

Our advisory board brings together experts from various sectors to provide strategic guidance and industry insights.
